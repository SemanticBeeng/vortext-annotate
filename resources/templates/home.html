Welcome to Spá

Summarizing evidence in bio-medical research is an immense undertaking, in part because unstructured PDF documents remain the main vehicle for disseminating scientific findings. Clinicians and researchers spend ages extracting information from these PDFs.
We introduce Spá—from the Old Norse word spá or spæ referring to prophesying—a web-based viewer that enables automated annotation and summarisation of PDFs via machine learning.
