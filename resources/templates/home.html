Welcome to Spá.

Summarizing evidence in bio-medical research is an immense undertaking, in part because unstructured PDF documents remain the main vehicle for disseminating scientific findings. Clinicians and researchers spend ages extracting information from PDFs.
We introduce Spá—from the Old Norse word spá or spæ referring to prophesying—a web-based viewer that enables automated data extraction from PDFs with machine learning.
